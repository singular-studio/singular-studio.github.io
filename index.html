Grow your business.

Whether you have yet to establish a web presence or just need to update your current website, Singular Studio can help. Building a website is crucial in today's world, where the vast majority of potential clients and customers use the internet in some form to inform the companies they engage with. Your website can be a simple informational one to direct customers to your brick and mortar business, or it can be a full ecommerce platform that forms the foundation of your company. Let's create a website that works for you!

What's the process?

It all starts with an idea

It doesn't matter how small or incomplete the idea is, or how grand and fully formed it might be. At Singular Studio, I will guide the project in the right direction, from concept to fully functoinal website. I believe in taking your ideas about how you want to represent your company and what you want to accomplish with a website, and providing my expertise to hone in on something that will allow your business to grow.

Discussion

After I have an idea of the direction you want to go, I will give you my expert opinion on what's possible and practical, or what's possible but impractical, or what's just plain impossible. My goal is to find a solution that is going to add the most amount of value to your business for the least amount of work. For every business there will be a solution that will work best, and it's important to come to an agreement about what that solution will be from the get go.

Mockups

Once we've reached a consensus for what we want in the website, I will proceed to produce mockups. These will provide you with something concrete to give you a good idea of how the finished product will look. This is the best time to make any major changes. If everything looks good, I will proceed with development of the website.

Your Website

You now have a complete website that will be visible to everyone in the world! At this point, minor changes can still be made, but major changes are often more difficult and can require starting from square one in many cases. But I'm confident this won't be an issue, as we will always strive to have a plan we can both be confident in, and always keep an open line of communication throughout the development process to ensure the final product is of the highest quality.
